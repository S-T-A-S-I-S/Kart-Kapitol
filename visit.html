First Time Here?

What to expect.

Kart Kapitol is built like an exhibit: curated, visitor-first, and easy to explore. You don't need to be a gamer — think of it like a live sports night with a modern twist.

  • Family-friendly vibes (depending on the event)
  • Live broadcast and production moments
  • Community + local partnership atmosphere
  • Great for visitors who want something different
  • Weekly Smash Bros tournaments — open to all
