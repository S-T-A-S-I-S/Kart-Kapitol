Why It Matters

Partnerships build the city.

Kart Kapitol exists to spotlight Marshalltown's esports ecosystem as a living portfolio. Partnerships turn events into visitor experiences — something the town can be proud of and share with the country.

  • Creates repeatable showcase weekends
  • Supports youth programs and education pathways
  • Builds local business visibility and involvement
  • Generates shareable media and town identity
  • Puts Marshalltown on the national esports map
