Exhibit Hall

Pick a wing. Enter the exhibit.

Each exhibit opens a curated display panel exploring a different dimension of Marshalltown's esports story.

Exhibit A: The Rise

How esports grew into a real community presence in Marshalltown — from first sparks to statewide recognition.

Exhibit B: The Arena

Venues, events, broadcasts, and watch parties — the live environments where esports becomes a shared experience.

Exhibit C: The People

Organizers, creators, volunteers, and the community that shows up behind the scenes every single time.

Exhibit D: The Impact

Education, careers, tourism, and measurable local value — esports as a force that extends beyond gameplay.

Exhibit E: Highlights

Rotating wall of moments: photos, clips, winners, and recaps from across the seasons.
